Harry Kane

Has played for Spurs since 2009 when he joined their Academy at the age of 16. The following year, he signed a contract with the First-team. Start of the 2013/2014 Season, he finally got his breakthrough and today he has grown to become one of the worlds best strikers.

Born: 28 July 1993 (London, England)
Christian Eriksen

Joined Spurs in 2013 and has been a key figure in their team ever since. Has an offensive playstyle that could fit in any of the top clubs around the world. Very creative player, and no doubt that he is one of the worlds best players in his position.

Born: 14 February 1992 (Middelfart, Denmark)
Dele Ali

Made his debut in start of 2015/2016 Season and has become one of the best younger playe. Good in both the defensive and offensive. Plays on a high quality level on the midfield, in both club and national team.

Born: 11 April 1996 (Milton Keynes, England)

About The Club

Tottenham Hotspur as a Club was formed in 1882 and turned professional in 1885 but didn't join the football league until 1908. Tottenham was the first club outside the football league to win the FA-Cup in 1901 since the formation of the league began in 1888. In recent years Tottenham has proven themself in the Premier League and in the Champions League that they have the capacity to win big titles.

The total team worth: $1,237 M
